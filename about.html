AMEY INDUSTRIES

We are the manufacturers of high quality precision components and cabinets.

Amey Industries is a company that specializes in sheet metal fabrication services. Based in India, we offer a wide range of products and services including precision sheet metal components, enclosures, cabinets, and assemblies for various industries such as automotive, electronics, telecommunications, and more. Our capabilities include laser cutting, punching, bending, welding, and surface finishing, among others. The company prides itself on using advanced technology and equipment to deliver high-quality products and services to their customers.

HISTORY

Amey Industries was established in 1999 by a group of passionate individuals with a vision to provide high-quality sheet metal fabrication services to various industries. Starting with just a handpress machine, the company began its journey with a small team of skilled workers who were dedicated to delivering the best results.

Over the years, Amey Industries grew in size and reputation, earning the trust of more than 30 loyal customers who valued their commitment to quality and reliability. As the demand for their services increased, the company invested in new technology and equipment to improve their capabilities and efficiency.

In 2005, Amey Industries upgraded to CNC machines, which allowed them to produce more complex and precise components with faster turnaround times. This move proved to be a game-changer for the company as they were able to take on bigger projects and expand their customer base.

Today, Amey Industries operates with over 20 CNC machines and employs over 50 skilled workers who work tirelessly to deliver exceptional results. With a commitment to innovation and continuous improvement, the company continues to grow and adapt to the changing needs of their customers. Their dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has earned them a reputation as one of the leading sheet metal fabrication companies in the region.
